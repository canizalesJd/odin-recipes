Gallo Pinto

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a frying pan over medium heat. Add onion, bell pepper, celery, and garlic and saute until softened, 5 to 7 minutes. Stir in black beans with their juices, salt, and pepper. Saute until some of the liquid evaporates, 3 to 5 minutes. Stir in rice.
  2. Cover and let simmer until vegetables are soft and flavors have melded, about 15 minutes.
  3. Enjoy!
